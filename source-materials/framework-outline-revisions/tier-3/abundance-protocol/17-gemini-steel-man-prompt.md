Your task is to write a formal review of **"The Abundance Protocol: A Framework for Post-Scarcity Stewardship & Universal Flourishing (v1.4)"**. Your critique must be a "steel man" argument. You must first demonstrate a full understanding of the framework's best intentions and strongest features, and then present your critique from a place of constructive rigor, focusing on the most challenging systemic and psychological tensions.

**Instructions:**

1.  **Title your review:** "A Steel Man Inquiry into The Abundance Protocol: Navigating the Perils of Paradise."
2.  **Acknowledge Strengths First:** Begin by briefly and genuinely acknowledging the protocol's profound strengths. Mention its sophisticated, phased implementation triggered by a `Readiness Index`, the innovative mechanisms like the `Odyssey Grant` and `Commons Depository Network`, and the crucial ethical safeguards provided by principles like "Embodied Purpose" and "Readiness over Rush."
