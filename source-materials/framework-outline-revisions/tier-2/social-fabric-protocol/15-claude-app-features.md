# Community Weaving App: Feature Specifications

## Core Philosophy
A privacy-first, community-sovereign platform that amplifies existing social bonds rather than replacing them with digital substitutes.

## Essential Features

### 1. Community Mapping & Discovery
- **Local Asset Discovery**: Map community skills, resources, and "hidden weavers"
- **Need/Offer Matching**: Simple reciprocity matching (I need childcare â†” I offer garden help)
- **Cultural Resource Sharing**: Community-controlled database of local wisdom and practices
- **Offline Sync**: All mapping data stored locally, syncs when connected

### 2. Event Coordination
- **Gathering Organizer**: Templates for community meals, skill-shares, listening circles
- **RSVP & Coordination**: Simple event management with offline capability
- **Cultural Calendar Integration**: Respects traditional seasons, ceremonies, and practices
- **Accessibility Planning**: Built-in accessibility checklist and accommodation tracking

### 3. Reciprocity Engine
- **Time Banking**: Track and coordinate mutual aid without monetization
- **Skill Sharing Circles**: Organize teaching/learning exchanges
- **Resource Circulation**: Community tool libraries, food shares, childcare chains
- **Gratitude & Recognition**: Non-monetized appreciation system (like Love Ledger)

### 4. Community Health Dashboard
- **LMCI Tracking**: Community-designed surveys and metrics
- **Participation Analytics**: Who's engaged, who might need outreach
- **Network Visualization**: See bonding vs bridging relationships
- **Conflict Early Warning**: Simple indicators for community tension

### 5. Weaver Support Tools
- **Training Modules**: Progressive curriculum for Community Weavers
- **Facilitation Guides**: Templates for restorative circles, visioning sessions
- **Peer Network**: Connect weavers across communities for mutual support
- **Resource Library**: Cultural adaptations, icebreakers, conflict resolution tools

### 6. Inter-Community Coordination
- **Regional Networks**: Connect neighboring communities for resource sharing
- **Knowledge Exchange**: Share innovations and lessons learned
- **Crisis Mutual Aid**: Coordinate support during emergencies
- **Collective Action**: Organize around shared issues (climate, housing, etc.)

## Technical Architecture

### Privacy & Sovereignty
- **Community-Owned Data**: Each community controls their own data
- **Mesh Networking**: Peer-to-peer connections reduce centralized control
- **Encryption**: End-to-end encrypted messaging and data storage
- **Right to Disconnect**: Communities can go offline and maintain functionality

### Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Multi-Modal Interface**: Voice, text, visual, gesture-based inputs
- **Language Adaptation**: Real-time translation + cultural context
- **Low-Bandwidth Design**: Works on 2G networks and basic smartphones
- **SMS Integration**: Core functions available via text message

### Cultural Responsiveness
- **Customizable Workflows**: Communities adapt features to their practices
- **Protocol Compliance**: Respects traditional governance and ceremony
- **Elder Authority**: Option for elder approval of community decisions
- **Sacred Boundary Protection**: Features can be disabled for cultural reasons

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: MVP (Months 1-6)
- Basic event coordination and reciprocity matching
- Simple community directory
- Offline-first architecture
- 3-5 pilot communities

### Phase 2: Network Effects (Months 6-12)
- Inter-community coordination features
- Basic metrics and health dashboards
- Weaver training integration
- 20-30 communities

### Phase 3: Advanced Features (Months 12-18)
- AI-assisted matching and facilitation
- Advanced network analysis
- Integration with GGF Digital Commons
- 100+ communities

## Success Metrics
- **Community Adoption**: % of community members actively using
- **Reciprocity Facilitated**: Number of successful exchanges/connections
- **Event Participation**: Increased attendance at community gatherings
- **Network Density**: Growth in inter-community relationships
- **Weaver Support**: Reduced burnout and increased effectiveness

## Integration with Social Fabric Framework
- **Phase Gates**: App metrics feed into LMCI calculations
- **Weaver Training**: Digital curriculum complements in-person training
- **Third Places**: Virtual spaces complement physical gathering spaces
- **Global Commons**: Data feeds into GGF ecosystem (with community consent)
- **Hearts Economy**: Future integration with AUBI Layer 2 rewards

## Risk Mitigation
- **Digital Divide**: Offline functionality and SMS backup
- **Surveillance Concerns**: Community-controlled encryption and data sovereignty
- **Cultural Appropriation**: Community customization and elder oversight
- **Platform Dependence**: Open source code and community ownership
- **Metric Gaming**: Qualitative validation and peer verification
