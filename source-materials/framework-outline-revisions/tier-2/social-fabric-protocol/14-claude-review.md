## The Case for a Community Weaving App

I think developing an app could be incredibly valuable, especially if designed with the framework's principles in mind:

**Potential Benefits:**
- **Scale coordination** between neighboring communities and regional networks
- **Support rural/remote areas** where physical gathering is challenging
- **Enable diaspora participation** in homeland community weaving
- **Facilitate skill-sharing** and reciprocity matching across distances
- **Track LMCI metrics** and community health indicators
- **Connect hidden weavers** across communities for mutual support

**Design Principles (aligned with the framework):**
- **Community sovereignty** - communities control their data and participation
- **Offline-first** - works without internet, syncs when available
- **Accessibility-first** - works on basic phones, multiple languages, screen readers
- **Privacy-by-design** - implements the Anti-Surveillance Covenant
- **Cultural adaptability** - communities can customize features and workflows
