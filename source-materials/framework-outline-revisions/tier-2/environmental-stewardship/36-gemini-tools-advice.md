Gemini:
Of course. This is a common and important challenge in a project of this scale: showing the complete vision without creating dead links or overpromising on content that isn't drafted yet.

The best approach is to frame the tools as part of a **living, evolving library**. This manages expectations honestly while still communicating the full, impressive scope of the framework.

Here is how I would phrase and structure the "Implementation Tools" section in your new `index.md`. This approach clearly distinguishes between what is available now and what is planned, without requiring you to create all the content immediately.

-----

### Recommended Phrasing for `index.md`

#### **\<a id="implementation-tools"\>\</a\>An Evolving Toolkit for a Living System**

The Ecological Intelligence & Rights Layer is supported by a comprehensive and evolving library of implementation tools designed to translate its principles into practice. Some foundational tools are available now for immediate use, while others are in active development or planned as the GGF ecosystem matures. This section provides an overview of the complete toolkit.

*Tools marked as `[Available Now]` are ready for use. Others are part of the framework's development roadmap.*

**Core Protocols & Foundational Tools:**

  * **[Available Now] Sacred Seed Kit:** A complete package for launching interfaith and indigenous-led restoration initiatives.
  * **[Available Now] AI Consciousness Assessment Framework:** A structured methodology for evaluating AI systems to inform ethical governance decisions regarding rights and responsibilities.
  * **[Planned] Data-to-Reward Pipeline Protocol:** The core mechanism that automates the linkage between verified ecological health data and the issuance of `AUBI` rewards (`Hearts` and `Leaves`).
  * **[Planned] Rights Hand-Off Protocol:** The formal legal process for transferring recognized ecosystem and other non-human rights from this framework's `Dynamic Rights Spectrum` to the `Justice Systems Framework` for legal enforcement.

**Governance & Coordination Tools:**

  * **[Available Now] Dialogue Facilitation Scripts:** Practical guides for hosting interfaith, community, and cross-cultural dialogues on environmental ethics.
  * **[Planned] Cross-Council Coordination Charter:** Defines the specific roles and coordination procedures between the `Planetary Health Council (PHC)`, the `Fractal Labor Parliament (FLP)`, and the `Social Resilience Council`.
  * **[In Development] First 100 Days Playbook:** A practical guide with specific, immediate actions for BAZs, youth, Indigenous groups, and the private sector to begin implementation.

**Crisis & Risk Management Tools:**

  * **[Planned] Crisis Response Protocol:** A structured approach for mobilizing rapid funding and community-led assessment during climate disasters and other emergencies.
  * **[In Development] Counter-Messaging Guide:** A resource for addressing resistance and misinformation with evidence-based communication strategies.
  * **[Planned] Specific Technology Governance Protocols:** A suite of protocols to be commissioned from the `Technology Governance (TGIF)` and `Biodiversity` frameworks to address emerging risks in biotech, nanotech, quantum computing, and ocean health.

-----

### Why This Phrasing Works:

  * **Manages Expectations:** The introductory paragraph and the `[Status]` tags clearly and honestly communicate that the toolkit is a work in progress.
  * **Shows the Full Vision:** It allows you to list all the critical new tools (like the `Data-to-Reward Pipeline`), demonstrating the framework's depth and integration without needing to link to a non-existent page.
  * **Creates a Roadmap:** It implicitly shows users what is coming next, turning a content gap into a development roadmap.
  * **Lowers Your Workload:** You only need to ensure the links for `[Available Now]` tools are active. For the others, you can simply leave them as unlinked text for now.

This approach allows your `index.md` to be both an accurate reflection of the current state and a compelling preview of the framework's complete potential, all without creating extra work for you at this moment.
