### **New Framework Draft: The Sanctuary Accord (Physical/Temporal)**

**Tier:** 2 (Specialized Application)
**Parent:** Mental Health & Social Resilience
**Mandate:** To protect the **Metabolic Integrity** of human consciousness by establishing zones free from extraction, commerce, and digital interruption.

**Pillar 1: The Right to Disconnect (Temporal Sanctuaries)**
* **Labor Protections:** Absolute prohibition on work communications during designated rest periods (The "Union Weekend" upgrade).
* **The Digital Sabbath:** Protocols for critical infrastructure to "cycle down" or minimize notifications during designated planetary rest times.

**Pillar 2: The Physical Sanctuary (Spatial Zones)**
* **The "Vila-vagnen" Protocol:** Mandate for public transit systems to maintain "Analog-Only" spaces.
* **The Third Place Protection:** Tax incentives and zoning laws for spaces where *loitering is legal and free* (Libraries, Parks, Plazas). Protection against the privatization of silence.
* **Advertising-Free Zones:** Prohibition of digital signage and attention-capture mechanisms in designated public nature reserves and heritage sites.

**Pillar 3: The Attention Defense Shield**
* **Algorithmic Limits:** Restrictions on "Infinite Scroll" and "Auto-Play" features in public-facing interfaces.
* **Cognitive Environmental Impact Statements:** New urban developments must assess their "Cognitive Load" on citizens.
