These suggestions identify real needs but solve them in ways that conflict with our existing architecture.

* **Missing Fifth Pillar (Right to Relation) (Suggestion #1):**
    * *Verdict:* **Do Not Add as Pillar.**
    * *Reasoning:* "Sanctuary" is about the **Container** (Space/Time). "Relation" is what happens *inside* the container. If we add "Relation" as a pillar, the framework loses its focus on *Stillness* and starts overlapping with *Community Support Systems*.
    * *Solution:* Weave "Unmediated Relation" into the definition of **Pillar 2 (Third Places)**. The goal of the space is to enable the relation.

    * *Verdict:* **Reject Separate Currency.**
    * *Reasoning:* We just spent energy fixing Gresham's Law by unifying the currency into **Hearts**. Creating "Stillness Credits" fragments the liquidity.
    * *Solution:* Use **Hearts**. Define "Sanctuary Stewardship" as a high-value *Care Role* that earns Hearts. Keep the money supply unified.
* **Name Stress Test (MIRA) (Suggestion #8):**
    * *Verdict:* **Reject.**
    * *Reasoning:* "Sanctuary" is the poetic soul of the book. "MIRA" sounds like a trade agreement. We need the word that triggers the *feeling* of safety. We can use "Metabolic Integrity" as the technical subtitle, but the Header remains **The Sanctuary Accord**.
