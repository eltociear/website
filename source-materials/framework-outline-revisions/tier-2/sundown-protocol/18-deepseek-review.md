### **Evaluation & Final Recommendations for the Sundown Protocol (Version 0.7)**  
**Grade: A+ (96/100)**  
*A near-flawless framework for just transitions, combining enforceable mandates, deep participatory governance, and visionary reparative justice. Minor refinements could optimize crisis resilience and granular metrics.*  

---

### **Strengths**  
1. **Holistic Justice Mechanisms:**  
   - Worker covenants (100% wage bridge, lifetime healthcare) and **Sacred Site Remediation Guidelines** set a global benchmark.  
   - **Seven-Generation Impact Assessments** and **Youth Council Veto** institutionalize intergenerational equity.  

2. **Enforceable Architecture:**  
   - Binding **BHI thresholds**, **asset seizures**, and **FPIC 2.0 veto power** prevent greenwashing and delay tactics.  

3. **Participatory Innovation:**  
   - **Labor-Driven Innovation Fund** and **Transition Solidarity Compacts** empower workers as co-designers.  
   - **Community Transition Boards** delegate real decision-making power to local stakeholders.  

4. **Crisis Resilience:**  
   - **Just Transition SWAT Team** and **Emergency Triage Measures** address abrupt collapses proactively.  

5. **Cultural Transformation:**  
   - **Ceremonial Closure Templates** and **Global Transition Story Archive** reframe industrial sunset as sacred composting.  

---

### **Targeted Improvements**  

#### **1. Strengthen Crisis Protocols**  
- **Problem:** Rapid industry collapse (e.g., oil price crash) may outpace triage measures.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Add **Pre-Negotiated Emergency Funds** (e.g., 10% of Legacy Transition Fund reserved for crises).  
  - Expand **sanctuary pathways** to include temporary housing in BAZs for displaced workers.  

#### **2. Refine Success Metrics**  
- **Problem:** Some metrics (e.g., `metric_lmci`) lack granularity for cross-regional comparison.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Add **disaggregated dashboards** (by gender, Indigenous status, disability) to the **Community Resilience Dashboard**.  
  - Define **Worker Satisfaction Trajectory** thresholds (e.g., ≥80% satisfaction for Phase 3 completion).  

#### **3. Optimize Financial Flows**  
- **Problem:** **Transition Bonds** may face market skepticism without AAA-rated backstops.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Partner with **GFANZ (Glasgow Financial Alliance for Net Zero)** to underwrite bonds.  
  - Add **Sovereign Guarantee Protocol** for Global South nations.  

#### **4. Deepen Indigenous Leadership**  
- **Problem:** **FPIC 2.0** lacks explicit sanctions for corporate interference.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Add **Blacklisting Mechanism**: Violators barred from GGF-aligned trade via `framework_gaian_trade`.  

#### **5. Clarify Jurisdictional Gaps**  
- **Problem:** Non-GGF states may host "transition havens" for fleeing industries.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Leverage **Digital Justice Tribunal** to freeze offshore assets of non-compliant entities.
