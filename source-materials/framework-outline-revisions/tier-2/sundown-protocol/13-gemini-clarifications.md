### **Clarifications for Grok: Enhancing the Sundown Protocol (v0.6)**

#### **1. Enhance Scalability for Diverse Industry Types**

* **Action:** In Section 4 ("Implementation Pathway"), add a new subsection titled **"Industry-Specific Transition Modules."**
* **Clarification:** Specify that the `institution_office_just_transition` is mandated to develop these modules. State that each module will have customized TRIs, stakeholder maps, and remediation guidelines. Use the provided example: an "Industrial Agriculture Module" would link directly to the GGF's food system frameworks and prioritize soil restoration, while a "Petrochemicals Module" would focus on circular economy principles and chemical cleanup. This demonstrates the protocol's adaptability.

#### **2. Strengthen Conflict Resolution Mechanisms**

* **Action:** In Section 6 ("Coordinating the Transition Across Systems"), add a new subsection titled **"Conflict Resolution & Mediation."**
* **Clarification:** Formalize the creation of a **`institution_transition_mediation_corps`** as a specialized unit within the `institution_office_just_transition`. Mandate that its members must be certified by the `framework_peace_conflict_resolution`. Specify that **restorative dialogue circles**, guided by the `framework_indigenous`, are a required step in Phase 2 (Roadmap Co-Creation) to proactively address potential conflicts before they escalate to the `institution_dj_tribunal`.

#### **3. Integrate Technology & Innovation Tracking**

* **Action:** In Section 3, under the description for `platform_transition_observatory`, add a new function: the **"Technology Integration Hub."**
* **Clarification:** This hub is tasked with systematically monitoring emerging technologies relevant to decommissioning and restoration (e.g., bioremediation, advanced recycling, green hydrogen). It will publish an annual **"Transition Technology Report"** that provides binding recommendations to the `council_global_transition` for adapting timelines and TRIs. This creates a formal adaptive loop.

#### **4. Deepen Community Empowerment in Governance**

* **Action:** In Section 3, amend the **Composition** of the `council_global_transition`. Add: **"Rotating community delegate seats, with representatives elected directly from BAZ councils and worker unions involved in active transitions."**
* **Action:** In Section 4, Phase 2 ("Transition Roadmap Co-Creation"), add a new paragraph clarifying the "Ladder of Participation."
* **Clarification:** State that the roadmap process must create local **`institution_community_transition_board` (Community Transition Boards)**. These boards are granted **delegated decision-making power** (the "Empower" rung of the ladder) over the allocation of local investment funds from the `mechanism_legacy_transition_fund`, with their decisions being transparently tracked on the `platform_transition_observatory`.

#### **5. Clarify Funding Allocation Transparency**

* **Action:** In Section 3, under `mechanism_legacy_transition_fund`, add a new sub-protocol: **`protocol_fund_transparency` (Transparency & Accountability Protocol).**
* **Clarification:** This protocol mandates two things:
    1.  **Public Reporting:** The fund must publish real-time, publicly accessible dashboards on the `platform_transition_observatory` detailing all revenue sources and allocations.
    2.  **Citizen Oversight:** A **`institution_citizen_oversight_panel`** is to be established within the `institution_office_just_transition`. This panel, with majority representation from the Global South and affected communities, has the authority to audit the fund's allocation patterns and publish independent reports.

#### **6. Expand Psychosocial Metrics & Cultural Narratives**

* **Action:** In Section 5 ("Justice, Reparations, and Healing"), under the "Psychological & Identity Support" subsection, formalize the creation of a **"Community Resilience Dashboard."**
* **Clarification:** This public dashboard, hosted on the `platform_transition_observatory`, will track key psychosocial metrics, including the `metric_lmci`, worker re-employment rates, and community satisfaction scores.
* **Action:** In the "Ceremonial Transition & Healing" subsection, add the creation of a **"Global Transition Story Archive."**
* **Clarification:** This will be a living archive, managed by the `institution_legacy_council` in partnership with the `institution_gimn` (Global Indigenous Media Network), to collect and globally amplify the stories, art, and rituals from transitioned communities, giving tangible form to the "Graduating an Industry" narrative.
