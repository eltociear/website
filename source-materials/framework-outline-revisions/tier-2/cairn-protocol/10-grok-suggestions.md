## Operational & Implementation Enhancements

### 3. Develop a Scalability Blueprint for Diverse Contexts
The roadmap assumes a uniform progression across BAZs, but communities vary widely in resources, infrastructure, and cultural readiness. A tailored scalability plan would improve adoption.

- **Suggestion**: Add a **Scalability Blueprint** to the **Implementation Roadmap** (Section 7), detailing:

### 4. Strengthen Community Feedback Loops
While the **Emergent Governance Protocol** and **Rapid Cycle Learning** are included, the framework could better emphasize continuous community input to prevent top-down implementation.

- **Suggestion**: Add a **Community Feedback Mechanism** to Section 3 (Governance Architecture):

  - **Feedback Integration Protocol**: A process to escalate community insights to the Cairn Council via the **Global Recovery Weavers Network**.

## Technological & Data Enhancements

### 7. Enhance AI and Digital Tools with Ethical Safeguards
The **Digital Sobriety & Wellness Toolkit** is strong, but the use of AI (e.g., AI-Assisted Matching, Predictive Early Warning) needs clearer safeguards to prevent misuse or over-reliance.

- **Suggestion**: Add a **Digital Ethics Charter** to Section 4, specifying:

  - **User Education Module**: A brief training in Recovery Resilience Workshops on how to use and critique AI tools.
- **Integration Directive**: Ensure the charter is co-developed with the **Sanctuary Accord** and **Neuro-Ethics Review Board**.
- **GGF Alignment**: Align with the **Technology Governance Implementation Framework (TGIF)** for ethical tech standards.

- **Integration Directive**: Ensure compliance with the **Aurora Accord** for user sovereignty.
- **GGF Alignment**: Partner with the **Global Metrology Council** to standardize data formats across frameworks.

## Risk Mitigation & Resilience

- **Suggestion**: Add a **Resilience Contingency Plan** to Section 7 (Implementation Roadmap):
