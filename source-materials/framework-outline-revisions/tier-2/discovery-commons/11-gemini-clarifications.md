* **Action:** In Section 3, expand the `initiative_bridge_fellowships` into a broader **`initiative_capacity_sharing_network`**.
* **Clarification:** This network will formally coordinate pro bono technical support from high-resource institutions to low-resource partners, managed by the Regional Hubs.
