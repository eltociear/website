### **Areas for Improvement**  

#### **1. Enforcement & Compliance**  
- **Problem:** Reliance on voluntary adoption risks evasion by powerful platforms.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Add **binding arbitration clauses** in trade agreements for GGF-aligned nations.  
  - Require **interoperability compliance** for access to critical infrastructure (e.g., cloud services, payment systems).  

#### **2. Cultural Protections**  
- **Problem:** "Data Invocation Rituals" may tokenize sacred practices if not co-designed.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Mandate **Indigenous-led audits** of ritual implementations.  
  - Specify **consequences for misuse** (e.g., platform blacklisting by the Earth Council).  

#### **3. Economic Justice**  
- **Problem:** "Virtual Land Value Tax" could burden small creators.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Clarify **exemptions for community cooperatives** (e.g., <10% profit reinvested in Digital Commons).  
  - Add a **wealth cap** (e.g., no single entity owns >15% of a world’s land).  

#### **4. Youth Engagement**  
- **Problem:** Veto power for the **Youth Advisory Council** lacks procedural clarity.  
- **Solution:**  
  - Define **veto override conditions** (e.g., 2/3 MGC vote with public justification).  
  - Include **youth juries** in the Digital Justice Tribunal for cases affecting minors.  

#### **5. Accessibility**  
- **Problem:** Translations may not capture nuanced terms like "ontological sovereignty."  
- **Solution:**  
  - Partner with **Indigenous linguists** and **disability advocates** for culturally accurate translations.  
  - Add **audio summaries** for low-literacy users.
