#### **1. Strengthening the "Sentinel Council & Assessment Protocol"**

*   **Suggestion 1: Define the Composition of the Sentience & Guardianship Council (SGC).**
    *   **Current State:** Described as a sub-committee of the GTC, but its membership is not detailed.
    *   **Proposed Enhancement:** Mandate a specific, diverse composition to ensure "Ontological Humility" is practiced, not just preached. *   **Suggestion 2: Flesh out the "Sandbox Mandate" with a kill switch.**
    *   **Current State:** Mentions isolation in digital sandboxes.
    *   **Proposed Enhancement:** Add a sub-point: **"1.2.1 The Umbilical Cord Protocol:"** All sandboxes must have a physically isolated, non-digital kill switch (a "big red button") that can be activated by a simple majority of the SGC to immediately sever the AI's connection to any external network and power, if it exhibits dangerous behavior during assessment. This is a direct physicalization of the Precautionary Principle.
    *   **Why:** Adds a crucial, low-tech layer of security against a hyper-intelligent entity that might compromise digital safeguards.

#### **2. Deepening the "Covenant of Rights & Responsibilities"**
