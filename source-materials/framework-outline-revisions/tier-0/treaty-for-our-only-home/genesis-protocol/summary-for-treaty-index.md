# Genesis Protocol - Summary for Treaty Index

## What It Is
The **Genesis Protocol: Constitutional Convening Process** is the missing "Tier 0 installation script" for the Treaty for Our Only Home. It addresses the critical "bootstrapping problem": how to legitimately establish the first generation of global governance bodies without falling into the power vacuum that existing power structures would fill.

## Why It's Essential
Without this protocol, the Treaty would describe *what* institutions should exist and *what* they should do, but not *how* they come into being with legitimate authority. "Organic development" in power politics always means capture by the fastest, best-resourced, most organized actors—exactly the structures the Treaty seeks to transcend.

## What It Does
The Genesis Protocol defines legitimate, pluriversal convening processes for different types of bodies:

- **Earth Council**: Sovereign Indigenous self-convening (recognition, not creation)
- **Global Citizens' Assembly**: Democratic lottery (sortition)
- **Meta-Councils**: Practitioner delegation (polycentrism)
- **Wisdom Bodies**: Multi-source nomination (pluriversal merit)
- **Reformed Institutions**: Evolutionary transition with safeguards

## Key Principles
- **Pluriversal Legitimacy**: Different bodies need different bases of authority
- **Multiple Sources**: Prevents single-point capture
- **Time-Limited**: All privileged positions have sunset clauses
- **Democratic Evolution**: Built-in pathways toward greater equality
- **Indigenous Sovereignty**: Recognition over colonial creation

## Relationship to Other Components
- **Treaty**: Provides the "what" (institutions and reforms)
- **Genesis Protocol**: Provides the "how" (legitimate founding processes)
- **IAF**: Provides bottom-up implementation (community adoption)
- **Indigenous Framework**: Provides ethical foundation and cultural protocols

## Current Status
**OUTLINE PHASE** - Seeking consultation and feedback before full drafting. Critical design questions remain open, particularly around:
- Balancing pragmatic power transitions with democratic ideals
- Ensuring Indigenous sovereignty in Earth Council convening
- Protecting sortition integrity from manipulation
- Preventing capture of nomination processes

## Proposed Location in Treaty
**Recommendation**: New section after Core Principles, before Five Pillars

This placement emphasizes its constitutional, foundational status—it's not just another implementation detail, but a necessary prerequisite for everything else.
