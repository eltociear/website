- Suggest: Monitors subject to random audit by Citizens' Assembly subset

### 4. **Conflict Zone Protocols**
- Add specific annex for convening in active conflict areas

### 5. **Cultural Protocol Conflict Resolution**

- Some cultural practices may genuinely conflict - need clear resolution authority

- Recommend: Genesis Protocol amendments require:
  - 2/3 Earth Council + 2/3 Citizens' Assembly + 2/3 Youth Council

- Makes it amendable but extremely difficult - preserves constitutional stability
