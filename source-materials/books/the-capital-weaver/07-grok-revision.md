# Book Outline: "The Capital Weaver"
***A Practical Guide to Regenerative Investing and the Dawn of a New Economy***

**Core Purpose:** To deliver a compelling, accessible, and practical guide to regenerative capital allocation that stands alone as a transformative resource for investors while serving as a gateway to the Global Governance Frameworks (GGF) ecosystem. The book introduces the "Capital Weaver" archetype and provides tools to operationalize this vision, culminating in an invitation to engage with the GGF as the ultimate regenerative operating system.

### **Preface: The Investor's Dilemma**

* **The Hook:** Sarah, a seasoned investor, faces cognitive dissonance: her portfolio delivers ROI but fuels crises—displaced communities, degraded ecosystems, eroded trust. She seeks alignment with a thriving world.  
* **The Stakes:** The polycrisis demands a new capital allocation model. The GGF offers a regenerative economy where investments heal, delivering resilient returns via systemic well-being.  
* **The Reader Promise:**  
  - *Philanthropists*: Fund systemic change with lasting impact.  
  - *Impact Investors*: Balance profit and regeneration.  
  - *Community Funds*: Build resilient local economies.  
  - *Family Offices*: Craft legacies that steward the commons.  
  This book equips you to become a "Capital Weaver," with tools and a path to the GGF.
