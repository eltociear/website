Case study: Amazon deforestation driven by growth-centric policies (2022: 11,000 km² lost). Data: IPCC reports linking rigid economic models to climate acceleration.

Examples: Social media echo chambers amplifying misinformation (e.g., 2020 election disinformation). Case study: Algorithmic polarization in U.S. elections (Pew: 70% of Americans see partisan divides growing). Data: Misinformation spread costing $78B annually in global economic impact (2021 study).
