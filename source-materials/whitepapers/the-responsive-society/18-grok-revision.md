It is a **boat builder's manual**: principles for redesigning our collective vessel (society) to be flexible, resilient, and responsive to the river’s flow. The GGF frameworks are practical tools for this boat, fostering capacities for skillful navigation without claiming to control or define the journey’s ultimate nature.
