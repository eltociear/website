## Remaining Analytical Concerns

**Implementation Complexity vs. Systemic Change**: While the systemic acupuncture argument justifies the *goal* of the complexity, it doesn't resolve whether the specific implementation pathway (BAZ councils, Community Weavers, four-layered resilience frameworks, bioregional federations) is the most effective way to achieve these goals.
