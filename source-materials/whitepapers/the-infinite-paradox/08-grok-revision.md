## **The Infinite Paradox: Non-Dual Ethics for Planetary Governance**

**Abstract**: A climate diplomat must act with moral conviction to secure a vital agreement, yet must also hold the humility to know her solution may have unintended consequences in a complex planetary system. This is the infinite paradox: the need for simultaneous ethical commitment and ontological humility. Current governance systems, built on assumptions of separation and certainty, force a false choice between rigid fundamentalism and paralyzing relativism. Grounded in the methodological rigor of *Beyond Fragmented Truth*, this paper offers a framework for resilient, adaptive governance that honors both moral clarity and the mystery of existence.
