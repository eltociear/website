**2. What Are 'Leaves'? An Economy for Stewardship**

* **Simple Definition:** Define Leaves in one clear sentence. "Leaves are a digital token earned by individuals and communities for verified actions that heal and regenerate our natural world."

**3. How It Works: From Action to Recognition**

* This section explains the mechanics in simple, non-technical steps.
* **Step 1: The Regenerative Act:** A person, a co-op, or a community group undertakes a project.

* **Step 2: The Verification:** The work is verified by a local, trusted, community-led body (you can call it a "Bioregional Stewardship Council"). This isn't a top-down bureaucracy but a group of local stakeholders.
* **Step 3: The Reward:** Once verified, the contribution generates Leaves in the participants' digital wallets.
* **The Digital Proof (NFTs Explained Simply):** "Each Leaf is a unique digital certificate—like an NFT—that acts as a permanent, transparent record of a specific ecological contribution. One Leaf might represent ten trees planted and thriving; another might represent an acre of restored wetland."
