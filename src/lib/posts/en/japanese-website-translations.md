**Japanese translation of Global Governance Frameworks**

The website now exist in Japanese, thanks to community contributor eltociear who translated 107 files without being asked.

This marks the first language expansion beyond Swedish and English—someone in Japan found the constitutional and governance frameworks website compelling enough to invest substantial effort making them accessible to Japanese speakers.

The translation includes:
- Complete pages translations

This is the open-source strategy working as intended: instead of pitching to translators, the work itself attracted contribution. The frameworks are beginning to have life beyond Stockholm.

No formal coordination was needed. They saw the repository, recognized value, and acted. This is what legitimate global governance looks like in practice—voluntary collaboration across borders based on shared recognition of necessity.

The Japanese version is now merged and live at globalgovernanceframeworks.org.
