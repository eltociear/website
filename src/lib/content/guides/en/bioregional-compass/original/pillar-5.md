## ## Pillar 5: Installing the Operating System - Core GGF Framework Integration

Your BAZ now has a vision, a map, a legal form, and a governance structure. ### **Step 1: Integrate the "Golden Triangle"**

The stability and legitimacy of your BAZ are anchored by its alignment with the three foundational GGF frameworks. This should be formally ratified by your Stewardship Council and written into your charter.

* **Align with The Treaty for Our Only Home:** Formally adopt the Treaty's core principles in your charter. This includes recognizing the **Rights of Nature** and embedding the legal principles of the **Ecocide Law** into your BAZ's own environmental regulations. This provides a powerful legal and ethical foundation for your stewardship of the bioregion.

* **Embody the Indigenous & Traditional Knowledge Framework:** While your governance is already grounded in Indigenous wisdom (Pillar 4), this step is about operationalizing it. Formally adopt **FPIC 2.0** as the mandatory protocol for all major decisions and establish your BAZ's relationship with the global **Earth Council** for moral and ethical guidance.

* **Connect to the Meta-Governance Framework:** "Plugging in" to the GGF network means participating in its coordination. Appoint your first delegates to represent your BAZ at regional **Meta-Governance Councils**. This ensures your community's voice is heard at a larger scale while maintaining full local autonomy under the principles of **subsidiarity** and **polycentrism**.

---
### **Step 2: Activate the Epistemic Commons**

A thriving community depends on a healthy information ecosystem. This step involves setting up the local infrastructure for truth, trust, and transparent communication.

* **Launch a Community Media Node:** Establish a local media outlet (a website, a podcast, a community radio station) that is chartered under the principles of the **`Synoptic Protocol`**. This node will be responsible for sharing information about the BAZ's governance and for providing the community with high-quality, non-polarizing media.

* **Implement the Digital Commons:** Set up your BAZ's instance of the **`Digital Commons Framework`**. This will be your community's transparent, secure, and sovereign platform for:
    * Storing official records (charter, meeting minutes, council decisions).
    * Hosting community dialogues.
    * Ensuring your community's data is owned and controlled by the community itself.

---
### **Step 3: Prepare for the Regenerative Economy**

This final step is the bridge to Pillar 6. Before you can fully launch your BAZ's regenerative economy, you need to have the technical and social infrastructure in place.

* **Set up Love Ledger Accounts:** Onboard your community members to the **`Love Ledger`**, creating the digital wallets that will allow them to receive **Hearts** and **Leaves** for their contributions to community and ecological well-being.
* **Educate and Onboard:** Host community workshops to explain the principles of the **`AUBI Framework`** and **`Work in Liberation`**. This social preparation is crucial for a smooth transition to a new economic model.
