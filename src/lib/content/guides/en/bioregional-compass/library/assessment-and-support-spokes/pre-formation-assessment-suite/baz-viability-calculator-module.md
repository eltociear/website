# BAZ Viability Calculator Module
*Comprehensive Feasibility Analysis for BAZ Formation*

---

The BAZ Viability Calculator provides communities with data-driven analysis of their readiness for BAZ formation. This module combines financial modeling, geographic assessment, and infrastructure analysis to generate a comprehensive viability score with actionable recommendations.

## Calculator Overview and Methodology

### Viability Assessment Framework

The calculator evaluates five critical viability dimensions using weighted scoring:

**Financial Viability (30% of total score)**
- Bridge economy funding adequacy
- Startup capital requirements
- Ongoing operational sustainability
- Revenue diversification potential
- Financial risk tolerance

**Geographic and Legal Context (25% of total score)**
- Location suitability for BAZ activities
- Regulatory environment assessment
- Community acceptance indicators
- Climate and ecological factors
- Land access and tenure security

**Human Capital and Community Readiness (20% of total score)**
- Essential skills availability
- Leadership capacity assessment
- Community cohesion indicators
- Volunteer commitment levels
- External support networks

**Infrastructure and Systems (15% of total score)**
- Physical infrastructure requirements
- Technology and communication needs
- Service delivery capacity
- Emergency preparedness systems
- Scalability potential

**Market and Economic Integration (10% of total score)**
- Local economy integration potential
- External market opportunities
- Economic resilience factors
- Supply chain considerations
- Regional economic context

### Scoring and Interpretation System

**Viability Score Ranges:**
- **85-100: High Viability** - Strong likelihood of successful BAZ formation with standard preparation
- **70-84: Moderate Viability** - Good potential with focused preparation in identified areas
- **55-69: Conditional Viability** - Possible with significant preparation and risk mitigation
- **40-54: Low Viability** - Major challenges requiring fundamental changes before formation
- **Below 40: Not Currently Viable** - Extensive preparation required before BAZ formation consideration

**Confidence Intervals:**
Each viability score includes confidence intervals based on:
- Data quality and completeness
- Assessment method reliability
- External uncertainty factors
- Community-specific risk factors
- Timeline and planning horizon
