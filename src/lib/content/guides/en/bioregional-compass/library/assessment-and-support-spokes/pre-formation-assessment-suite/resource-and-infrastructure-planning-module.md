# Resource and Infrastructure Planning Module
*Assessment of Physical, Financial, and Organizational Infrastructure Requirements*

---

The Resource Planning Module provides comprehensive analysis of infrastructure development needs for BAZ formation. This module evaluates physical, technological, financial, and organizational infrastructure requirements to generate realistic development timelines and resource allocation strategies.

## Infrastructure Planning Framework

### Comprehensive Infrastructure Assessment

The module evaluates infrastructure readiness across five integrated systems:

**Physical Infrastructure Systems (30% of infrastructure readiness score)**
- Land access, housing, and physical facilities
- Water, energy, and utilities systems
- Transportation and connectivity infrastructure
- Food production and processing facilities
- Waste management and recycling systems

**Technology and Communications Infrastructure (25% of infrastructure readiness score)**
- Digital communication and internet connectivity
- Information technology and data management systems
- Digital commons and knowledge management platforms
- Economic system technology (Hearts/Leaves platforms)
- Governance and decision-making technology tools

**Financial and Economic Infrastructure (20% of infrastructure readiness score)**
- Community treasury and financial management systems
- Alternative currency systems and exchange platforms
- Banking relationships and financial services access
- Investment and asset management systems
- Emergency financial reserves and risk management

**Social and Organizational Infrastructure (15% of infrastructure readiness score)**
- Governance systems and decision-making processes
- Conflict resolution and mediation systems
- Community education and skill development programs
- Healthcare and wellness service delivery systems
- Emergency response and crisis management capabilities

**Sustainability and Resilience Systems (10% of infrastructure readiness score)**
- Renewable energy and energy independence systems
- Climate resilience and adaptation infrastructure
- Ecological restoration and regenerative systems
- Emergency preparedness and disaster resilience
- Long-term sustainability planning and monitoring

## Section 1: Physical Infrastructure Assessment

- **Land Development Potential:** Assessment of development potential, infrastructure access, and improvement opportunities

*Housing and Shelter Infrastructure:*

## Section 6: Infrastructure Development Planning

### Infrastructure Implementation Strategy

**Phased Infrastructure Development Planning**

*Development Phase Prioritization:*

**Phase 1: Foundation Infrastructure (Year 1-2)**
- Essential utilities (water, power, internet)
- Basic housing and shelter adequacy
- Community treasury and financial systems
- Basic governance and communication systems
- Emergency preparedness and safety systems

**Phase 2: Community Systems Development (Year 2-4)**
- Community facilities and shared spaces
- Alternative economic system implementation
- Enhanced digital commons and technology systems
- Community services and support programs
- Sustainable systems and infrastructure

**Phase 3: Advanced Systems Integration (Year 3-5)**
- Advanced technology and automation systems
- Comprehensive sustainability and resilience systems
- Community enterprise and revenue generation
- Advanced governance and decision-making systems
- Regional integration and external partnerships

**Phase 4: Innovation and Optimization (Year 5+)**
- Innovation and experimental systems
- Advanced sustainability and regeneration
- Community wealth building and investment
- Regional leadership and model development
- Movement integration and knowledge sharing
