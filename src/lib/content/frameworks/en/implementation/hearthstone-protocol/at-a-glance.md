# The Hearthstone Protocol: At-a-Glance

**A Framework for Commons & Stewardship**

---

## **What Is It?**

The **Hearthstone Protocol** is humanity's blueprint for transitioning from extractive ownership to regenerative stewardship‚Äîreturning land, capital, and enterprise to the sacred trust of communities for the well-being of all beings and future generations.

**The Vision**: Legal pathways that transform property relations from "who owns what" to "who cares for what"‚Äîenabling Indigenous sovereignty, community stewardship, and planetary healing.

---

## **Why Now?**

**The Crisis**: Corporate extraction devastates ecosystems while communities lose sovereignty over their territories. Climate action requires massive asset transitions, but existing property law blocks the collective stewardship necessary for planetary healing.

**The Opportunity**: Converging crises create momentum for fundamental transformation while legal tools and cooperative models demonstrate viable alternatives to extractive ownership.

## **Legal Architecture**

### **New Legal Entities**
- **Stewardship Trusts**: Replace corporate title with community governance
- **Sacred Trust Designations**: Permanent protection for irreplaceable sites
- **Bio-Cultural Stewardship Trusts**: Co-governance where ecology and culture intertwine

### **International Institutions**
- **International Commons Court**: Enforce rematriation and prosecute commons violations
- **Office of Commons Transition**: Technical support and mediation for asset transitions
- **Commons Title Registry**: Blockchain tracking of stewarded assets with Love Ledger integration

---

## **Transition Pathways**

### **Land Rematriation Initiative**
Global Commons Fund purchases territories for Indigenous and community stewardship

### **Corporate Co-op Conversion**
Enterprises transition to Stewardship Trusts through incentivized voluntary processes

### **Urban Commons Transition**  
Cities convert public assets to community control with anti-gentrification safeguards

### **Capital Commons Transition**
Financial capital redirected to regenerative investment through tax incentives

---

## **Economic Integration**

### **Stewardship-Linked Valuation (SLV)**
Asset value tied to regenerative performance:
- **Biosphere Health Index (BHI)**: Ecological metrics
- **Love, Meaning, Connection Index (LMCI)**: Social well-being
- **Transition Buffer**: Gradual adjustment (10% below baseline, rising 2% annually)

### **Commons Dividend Protocol**
Surplus distribution: 40% reinvestment, 30% to stewards (Hearts/Leaves), 30% to Global Commons Fund

### **Hearts & Leaves Currencies**
- **Hearts**: Reward care work and community building
- **Leaves**: Reward ecological restoration and stewardship
- **Love Ledger**: Validates contributions through multi-stakeholder proof-of-care
