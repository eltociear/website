### 2. üè¢ **Community Providers Offer Essential Services**

### 3. üíö **Hearts Investment Creates Revenue**
Community members use their AUBI Hearts allocation to purchase services from Community Providers, creating sustainable revenue for meaningful work.

### 4. üè¶ **Hearts Treasury Enables Sustainability**
