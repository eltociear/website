## **Implementation Phases**

### **Pre-Mandate: Pathfinder Compact** (Voluntary)
- Companies voluntarily commit to proactive transition
- Receive planning support, preferential financing, leadership roles

### **Phase 0: Assessment** (Year 0)
- Regional readiness evaluation
- Community consent protocols
- Baseline resilience mapping

### **Phase 1: Designation** (Year 1)  
- **Planetary Health Council** triggers industry sunset based on ecological thresholds
- **Global Transition Council** ratifies binding timeline (e.g., 2040)

### **Phase 2: Roadmap Co-Creation** (Years 1-3)
- Multi-stakeholder convening (workers, communities, Indigenous peoples, youth)
- Binding **Transition Roadmap** created through restorative dialogue
- Companies achieving regenerative certification can exit protocol

### **Phase 3: Managed Decommissioning** (Years 3-15+)
- Asset decommissioning and ecosystem restoration
- Workforce retraining and community investment
- **Indigenous Monitoring Teams** ensure cultural protection

### **Phase 4: Asset Transfer** (Ongoing)
- Remediated assets transfer to community **Stewardship Trusts**
- Priority for rematriation to **Bioregional Autonomous Zones**
