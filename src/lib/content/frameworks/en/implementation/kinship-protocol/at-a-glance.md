# Kinship Protocol: At a Glance

## **What It Is**
The **Kinship Protocol** is a comprehensive Tier 3 framework within the Global Governance Framework that embeds gender and LGBTQ+ justice as a non-negotiable layer across all global governance systems. It transforms institutions from spaces of exclusion into engines of liberation, ensuring people of all genders and sexual orientations have institutionalized power in shaping our collective future.
