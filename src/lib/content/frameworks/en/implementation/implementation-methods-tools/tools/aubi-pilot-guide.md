# AUBI Pilot Program Guide
*Launching Abundant Universal Basic Income with Hearts, Leaves, and Right Relationship*

> *"True abundance flows not from accumulation but from relationship‚Äîwith each other, with the land, and with the sacred reciprocity that sustains all life. AUBI honors the wealth that already exists in our communities while creating new pathways for care and regeneration to flourish."*  
> ‚Äî Social Resilience Council, Indigenous Economic Wisdom Collaborative

## Tool Overview

The AUBI Pilot Program Guide provides comprehensive methodology for launching Adaptive Universal Basic Income systems that integrate traditional Indigenous economics with contemporary abundance technologies. This tool enables communities to pilot the Hearts and Leaves currency system while building capacity for regenerative economics based on Right Relationship rather than extraction.

**Essential for communities that are:**
- Transitioning from scarcity-based to abundance-based economic models
- Integrating care work, cultural preservation, and ecological stewardship into economic recognition
- Building community wealth and economic sovereignty through cooperative models
- Piloting Hearts and Leaves currency systems with Love Ledger technology
- Creating economic systems that serve seven-generation thinking and community wellbeing

**Right Relationship Foundation:** AUBI operates on the principle of Right Relationship‚Äîbalanced, regenerative connections between individuals, communities, and the planet. Traditional Indigenous gift economies provide the wisdom foundation for contemporary abundance technologies.

### **Common AUBI Pilot Challenges & Solutions**

#### **Challenge: Technology Overwhelming Traditional Economics**
**Problem**: Love Ledger and digital systems disrupting traditional economic relationships and cultural protocols

**Solutions:**
- **Traditional Authority Leadership**: Traditional knowledge keepers leading all technology integration and digital system governance
- **Cultural Protocol Priority**: Traditional economic protocols guiding technology use rather than technology determining economic relationships
- **Community Data Sovereignty**: Complete community control over digital systems preventing external control or cultural appropriation
- **Traditional Interface Design**: Technology adapted for traditional cultural preferences and elder accessibility
- **Analog Alternatives**: Non-digital participation options maintaining traditional economic relationships and cultural protocols

#### **Challenge: Individual Competition Undermining Community Cooperation**
**Problem**: AUBI creating individual competition for Hearts and Leaves rather than strengthening community cooperation

**Solutions:**
- **Traditional Reciprocity Emphasis**: Traditional gift economy and reciprocity principles guiding AUBI participation
- **Community Validation Priority**: Community-controlled validation ensuring AUBI strengthens rather than weakens traditional cooperation
- **Traditional Conflict Resolution**: Traditional mediation and healing methods addressing economic disputes and community concerns
- **Collective Recognition**: Group and community recognition through Hearts and Leaves rather than only individual recognition
- **Traditional Accountability**: Traditional community accountability methods ensuring AUBI serves community wellbeing rather than individual accumulation

#### **Challenge: External Funding Compromising Community Control**
**Problem**: External funding sources for AUBI pilot creating dependency or external control over community economic systems

**Solutions:**
- **Community Economic Sovereignty**: Clear community authority over all economic system decisions without external override
- **Traditional Resource Systems**: Maximizing traditional economic systems and community resources before external funding
- **Resource Sovereignty Protocols**: Clear agreements ensuring external resources do not compromise community control or traditional authority
- **Traditional Gift Economy**: External resources integrated through traditional gift economy protocols rather than market relationships
- **Democratic Accountability**: Community oversight of all funding relationships and external resource coordination
