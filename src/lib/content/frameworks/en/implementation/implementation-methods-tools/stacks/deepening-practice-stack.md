## <a id="stack-overview"></a>Stack Overview & Advanced Practice Philosophy: Beyond Traditional Governance

### **Who This Stack Serves**

**Primary Target Communities**:
- **Governance Innovation Leaders**: Communities with 3+ years of successful governance innovation and recognized excellence in democratic participation
- **Advanced Indigenous Communities**: Indigenous communities integrating traditional knowledge with cutting-edge technology while maintaining cultural sovereignty
- **Wisdom Communities**: Intentional communities integrating consciousness development, spiritual practice, and governance innovation
- **Academic and Research Institutions**: Universities and research centers pioneering governance innovation and consciousness research
- **Technology Communities**: Tech communities developing ethical AI, digital sovereignty tools, and consciousness-supporting technologies
- **Movement Leaders**: Organizations and networks leading movements for democratic transformation and consciousness evolution

**Prerequisites for Advanced Practice**:
- **Governance Mastery**: Demonstrated excellence in democratic governance with sophisticated participation systems and cultural integration
- **Innovation Track Record**: Successful governance innovations with documented impact and replication by other communities
- **Cultural Integration**: Deep integration of traditional knowledge and cultural practices with contemporary governance innovation
- **Technology Readiness**: Advanced technology infrastructure and community capacity for AI integration and digital sovereignty
- **Consciousness Development**: Community commitment to consciousness evolution and wisdom development alongside material coordination
- **Network Leadership**: Leadership role in regional networks and contribution to broader movements for democratic governance

**Not Recommended For**:
- Communities without basic governance mastery (use Getting Started Stack)
- Communities still building capacity (use Building Capacity Stack)
- Communities focusing on foundational democratic development (continue with Building Capacity Stack)
- Organizations without significant technology infrastructure and expertise
- Communities without commitment to consciousness development and traditional knowledge integration
