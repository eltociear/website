## What Starting Actually Looks Like

**Not:** Quit your job, move to Bristol, become full-time organizer
**Instead:** Monday morning, execute the first move

**The Week 1 Protocol:**

### Monday: Map Your Territory
- Open spreadsheet
- List: 3 institutions you could infiltrate (council, university, union, professional association)
- List: 5 people in your existing networks who could be Ring 1 (Discord mods, gym crew, work friends)
- List: 1 crisis in your city that creates political opening (housing, high street, mental health, youth services)

**Time required:** 2 hours
**Cost:** £0
**Risk:** Zero (you're just observing)

### Tuesday: Make First Contact
- Email one councillor about one thing (use template from Chapter 17)
- Or: Message one person from potential Ring 1 ("coffee this week?")

**Example email:**

> Subject: Community proposal - [Your Ward] Sanctuary Pilot
>
> Dear Councillor [Name],
>
> I'm a [your ward] resident interested in community-managed green spaces. I've seen successful sanctuary district models in Bristol and Preston that reduce council maintenance costs while improving mental health outcomes.
>
> Would you be open to 15-minute conversation about piloting similar approach in [location]?
>
> I have costings, precedents, and funding pathways if useful.
>
> Best,
> [Your name]

**Time required:** 15 minutes
**Cost:** £0
**Risk:** They ignore it (you try next councillor)

### Wednesday: Build Your Squad Foundation
- Coffee with potential Ring 1 member
- Don't pitch revolution
- Just: "I'm thinking about organizing around [issue]. Interested in being part of early conversations?"

**Time required:** 1 hour
**Cost:** £5 (two coffees)
**Risk:** They say no (you try next person)

### Thursday: Document and Research
- Start simple spreadsheet: contacts, responses, next steps
- Research your city's version of the problem (housing waiting list, mental health stats, high street vacancies)
- Find 1-2 examples of similar work elsewhere (Bristol CLT, Preston co-ops, Liverpool sanctuaries)

**Time required:** 2 hours
**Cost:** £0

### Friday: Identify Resources
- Google: "[Your city] community grants"
- Google: "[Your city] cooperative development"
- Google: "[Your city] community land trust"
- Bookmark what exists, note what's missing

**Time required:** 1 hour
**Cost:** £0

### Saturday-Sunday: Rest
- Seriously, rest
- This is marathon, not sprint
- Play Geoguessr
- Lift weights
- Moderate your Discord
- Live your life

**Sustainable pace beats heroic burnout.**
