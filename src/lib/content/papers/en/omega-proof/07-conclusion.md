## What the Diagnosis Revealed

The autopsy identified four terminal bugs that make this impossibility structural, not contingent:

1. **The Sovereignty Trap**: Binary territorial logic makes any ambiguous arrangement existentially threatening
2. **Resource Weaponization**: Strategic assets cannot be shared without triggering strategic defeat logic
3. **Relative-Gain Lock**: Great powers optimize for competitor disadvantage over absolute stability
4. **Free-Rider Incentive**: Actors profit from instability without penalty

These are not policy choices that can be reversed. They are load-bearing assumptions of the operating system. Patching is futile.
