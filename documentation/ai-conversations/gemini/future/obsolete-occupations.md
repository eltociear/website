- **Advertising executives** (manipulative marketing)  
- **Corporate lobbyists** (influence for harmful industries)  
- **Predatory lenders & payday loan operators**  
- **Private prison administrators**  
- **Weapons manufacturers & arms dealers**

Many bureaucratic or meaningless jobs exist only to sustain corporate structures:  
- **Middle managers in bloated hierarchies**  
- **Corporate compliance officers (if regulations simplify)**  
- **Redundant administrative roles in over-financialized sectors**  
- **Telemarketers & cold callers**  

### **3. Jobs Replaced by Automation & AI (with ethical oversight)**

- **Repetitive data entry clerks**

- **Toll booth operators (with smart infrastructure)**
