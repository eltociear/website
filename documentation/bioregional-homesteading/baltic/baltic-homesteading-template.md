## üåç Purpose of This Document

This is a **living knowledge base** created by and for homesteaders in the Baltic bioregion. 

**What makes this different from generic permaculture resources:**
- ‚úÖ **Bioregionally specific** - focused on what works *here* in Baltic climate/soil
- ‚úÖ **Peer-validated** - contributed by people with real experience
- ‚úÖ **Failure-inclusive** - we document what doesn't work, not just successes
- ‚úÖ **Timeline-realistic** - honest about how long things actually take
- ‚úÖ **Coordination-enabled** - connects you with experienced practitioners

**Current status:** Volunteer-driven knowledge sharing. No organization, no budget, no bureaucracy.

**Potential future:** If this proves valuable, it could expand to economic coordination (bulk purchasing, tool sharing), integration with other bioregions, or formalization as a cooperative. But we're starting simple: just sharing what works.

**How to use this:**
1. Find your country section (Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania)
2. Read what experienced homesteaders have learned
3. Contact contributors if you have specific questions
4. **Add your own learnings** as you gain experience

**How to contribute:**
- Request edit access from the coordinator
- Add your experiences to the relevant sections
- Be honest about failures and challenges
- Include timelines (Year 1, Year 3, Year 5, etc.)
- Optional: Add your contact info if you're willing to mentor others
